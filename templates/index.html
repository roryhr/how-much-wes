How Much Wes Anderson?

Wes Anderson has a unique aesthetic.
Do you think you can recognize his style?

One frame from the trailer of Wes Anderson's "The Grand Budapest Hotel".

Test it out with the upper image or with the lower image or upload your own image to see how much Wes Anderson it is.

A fine example from @accidentallywesanderson on Instagram.

How does it work?

In 2019 I trained a small CNN on a total of about 4000 training images pulled from the Wes Anderson trailers on YouTube along with some images from Instagram. The model was built following the tutorial Building powerful image classification models using very little data . In 2023 the model was updated to Tensorflow 2 following the latest transfer learning example.

This webapp was originally served on Heroku (pour one out) and is now served from the free tier of Fly.
